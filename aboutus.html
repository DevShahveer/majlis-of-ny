THE STORY OF MAJLIS

Majlis of New York:
A Premier Hookah Bar & Restaurant

Majlis of New York stands as a top-tier destination, offering a unique combination of premium halal hookah and cross-cultural Southern European cooking. With an atmosphere of refined grace, Majlis is the place where comfort meets elegance, creating the ideal location for memorable meetups.
