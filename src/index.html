Our Story

Where Tradition Meets Luxury

Majlis of New York, established in 2011 by Rizwan Siddiqui, is an outstanding Hookah Bar and Lounge that brings the lavish feel of Dubai and Europe to New York, a best spot for locals and foreigners, offering premium and outclass Halal Hookah experience and a special mix of cross-culture cuisine.
